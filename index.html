I am passionate about building relationships with people and using my communication skills to showcase my skills and leadership ability. During the course of my college career I have had many jobs and leadership opportunities in the various clubs that I am apart of to work on and build my professional skills. The clubs include Social Spear, AITP, STARS Alliance, WVFS Radio Club, Running Club at FSU, and THT. I worked all four years of college with jobs ranging from a Receptionist at the dorms on campus, an employee at one of the dining halls, and an IT tech help camp facilitator for high school kid’s. I enjoy reaching out and helping out the younger generation, I have performed several company outreach project’s in which I get companies to agree to work with Florida State, which means that they agree to hire and look at more Florida State Students. I have also done many activities to help high school kids ranging from taking them on tours of the campus to helping out an IT help camp on campus. In my free time you can find me studying in the library, hanging on Landis with my friends, hiking, working out with my friends, or watching Netflix. I know that I have the skills to succeed and am very excited to use these skills to excel in my professional career.
